EVENT BUZZ has been a trusted home for live music since 2007. From day one, we’ve set about making it as easy, fun and fair as possible for you to see your favorite artists live. Right now, more than 15 million music fans across the globe use Songkick to track their favorite artists, discover concerts and buy tickets with confidence.
We’ve always put fans first. That means no spam about things you don’t care about, and no inflated ticket prices. Live music should work for everyone, and we work hard to empower our community and cut out the BS along the way. Nothing but good vibes from here.
